URDC AT GLANCE

The University Research & Development (URD) Cell at CSJMU is a dynamic hub promoting innovation, research excellence, and industry collaboration. It provides administrative and managerial support for sponsored projects, consultancy, institutional research, minor grants, intellectual property processes, fellowships, and academic events. Focused on real-world challenges, the URD Cell fosters a culture of creativity, inquiry, and problem-solving among faculty and students while encouraging interdisciplinary and industry-linked research. The university supports research through well-defined policies, seed funding, scholarships, and access to shared facilities. CSJMU offers a well-equipped campus with modern academic infrastructure, IT-enabled labs, a central library, and extensive sports and residential facilities. Research support includes access to Ph.D. data via student portals, central instrumentation facilities, digital and print resources, plagiarism detection tools (Drillbit), Shodhganga e-theses, and over 13,000 journals through the ONOS initiative. Ph.D. admissions follow UGC Regulations 2022 and university ordinances. The URD Cell is introducing an AI-integrated research governance framework to enhance supervision, ethical compliance, and research monitoring, positioning CSJMU as a leader in transparent and technology-driven research ecosystems.

Dr. Namita Tiwari

It gives me immense pride to present Volume 2, Issue 1 of the CSJMU Research Bulletin, a reflection of our university’s continuous journey towards research excellence, innovation, and academic leadership. This edition of the Research Bulletin showcases the diverse and impactful research contributions emerging from various disciplines, highlighting the university’s growing presence in national and international academic platforms. The steady rise in research publications, interdisciplinary collaborations, and quality outcomes reflects our collective commitment to excellence and innovation. The introduction of progressive policies, structured monitoring mechanisms, and technology-driven research governance has significantly enhanced the quality, relevance, and ethical standards of research at CSJMU. Initiatives such as research progress reviews, proposal evaluations, fellowship support, and AI-driven research schemes are creating an enabling environment for scholars to pursue meaningful and application-oriented research. Our focus is steadily shifting from publications to impactful outcomes, encouraging innovation, product development, and real-world problem-solving. The CAM-AI Research Projects Scheme and interdisciplinary initiatives further strengthen this vision by promoting cutting-edge research aligned with societal and industrial needs. This bulletin also reflects the inclusive and participative research culture at CSJMU, where faculty, scholars, and affiliated colleges actively contribute to knowledge exchange and academic growth. Such collective efforts are instrumental in building a vibrant and globally competitive research ecosystem. I extend my sincere appreciation to the entire R&D team, faculty members, research scholars, and contributors whose dedication and hard work have made this publication possible. I am confident that this initiative will continue to inspire excellence, foster innovation, and strengthen the research culture of our university. Let us continue to innovate, collaborate, and contribute meaningfully to society through research.
